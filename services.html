Wipers

Ltd. supply wipers to a number of markets which include India,Africa, kenya, Cambodia, Thailand, Italty, Europe and many more countries.We understand the competitive nature of the wiper industry and know what it takes to lead the pack.Our wipers come in packing of 20, 45, and 350 kilogram bales.

Features:

  • Color T-Shirt Cut: This is basically t-shirt material and due to its versatility of absorption, durability and cleaning prowess is used across all industries.
  • Sweat Cut: This particular grade has great absorption capabilities and are largely used in Industries where grease and oil is used.
  • Mix Cotton: This grade is normally used in most of the industries barring places where heavy and rough usage is required.
