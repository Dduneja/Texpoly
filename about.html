About Us

Texpoly Impex Pvt Ltd was established in the year 2014 & is the leading company of Kandla Special Economic Zone, India.

Texpoly Impex Pvt Ltd is involved in all facets of the textiles recycling market from the collection of used clothing or secondhand clothing to the reuse of this used clothing. We sort approximately 1,00,000 lbs of used clothing every day in our 2,00,000 square foot warehouse located in KSEZ, India. We are biggest exporter to Africa, kenya, Cambodia, Thailand, Italty, Europe and many more countries.We are also the biggest exporters of USA.
